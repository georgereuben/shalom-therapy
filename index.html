About Me

At Shalom Therapy, I believe in a holistic approach to mental health that addresses the interconnected aspects of mind, body, and spirit. My practice is founded on the principle that true healing comes from understanding the whole person, not just treating symptoms.

As a licensed therapist, I combine evidence-based therapeutic methods with compassionate care to create a safe, supportive environment where growth and healing can occur. I am committed to helping my clients develop the insights and skills they need to overcome challenges and live more fulfilling lives.

Whether you're struggling with anxiety, depression, relationship issues, or life transitions, I'm here to support you on your journey to wellness and personal transformation.

My Services

Individual Therapy

Personalized therapy sessions tailored to address your unique needs and goals for mental health and personal growth.

Anxiety Treatment

Learn effective strategies to manage anxiety and reclaim a sense of calm and control in your life.

Depression Therapy

Find hope and healing through evidence-based approaches to treating depression and improving mood.

Trauma Recovery

Gentle, trauma-informed therapy to help you process past experiences and move forward with healing.

Stress Management

Develop practical tools and techniques to reduce stress and enhance your overall wellbeing.

Life Transitions

Support through significant life changes, helping you navigate uncertainty and find new direction and purpose.
