Meet Our Therapists

Manju George

Counselling Psychologist

Manju is a Counselling Psychologist with expertise in guiding individuals through emotional and personal growth challenges. At Shalom Therapy, she believes that true healing comes from understanding the whole person, not just treating symptoms.

With advanced degrees in Counselling and Developmental Psychology from the University of Kent, UK and certifications in Person-Centered Psychotherapy and Special Needs Education, she offers a compassionate, client-focused approach that empowers you to discover your own solutions.

She provides a safe, supportive environment where you can explore your thoughts and feelings without judgment, developing the insights and skills needed to overcome challenges and live a more fulfilling life. Her online therapy sessions are available to clients in Kochi and throughout India.

Manushi Parwani

Counselling Psychologist & Life-Skills Trainer

Manushi is a counselling psychologist and life-skills trainer with expertise in providing safe spaces for emotional exploration and growth. With an M.Sc. in Counselling Psychology from CHRIST (Deemed to be) University, Delhi-NCR, and certification as a Person-Centered Therapist, she has trained under Meenakshi Kirtane (Director Psychodrama Institute of India and Maanas- The Inside Story).

Her therapeutic approach is deeply-rooted in the Rogerian belief of humanistic approach and empathy. She uses an eclectic approach in her sessions, incorporating Person-centered Therapy and Solution-focused approach at its core. She has worked with clients ranging from 9 years old to 45 years old, with particular expertise in substance use issues, relationship issues, and anger management.

If you are looking for a safe space to share and explore your thoughts, feelings and emotions, Manushi provides compassionate, client-focused support to help you navigate life's challenges.

My Services

Individual Therapy

Personalized therapy sessions using a person-centered approach that respects your unique perspective and empowers you to discover your own solutions for mental health and personal growth.

Anxiety & Stress Management

Evidence-based techniques including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and mindfulness interventions to help manage anxiety, reclaim calm, and develop effective stress management strategies.

Self-Esteem & Personal Growth

Supportive guidance to address low self-esteem and build confidence, using motivational techniques and resilience training to help you recognize your worth and potential.

Grief Counselling

Compassionate support through the grief process, helping you navigate loss and find healing with empathetic listening and specialized grief counselling techniques.

Relationship Counselling

Guidance for improving interpersonal relationships, enhancing communication skills, and developing healthier connections with others through increased self-awareness and empathy.

Special Needs Support

Specialized assistance for children with exceptional needs and their families, using individualized strategies developed through extensive experience in special education.
